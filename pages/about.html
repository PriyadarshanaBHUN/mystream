About MyStream

MyStream is a cutting-edge cinematic platform that allows users to explore a vast library of movies and TV shows from various genres, countries, and time periods. Whether you're looking for the latest blockbuster or an old classic, MyStream brings you a wide selection with detailed information, including full descriptions, ratings, genres, posters, and release dates. Our goal is to provide movie lovers with an immersive, interactive experience where discovering new content is not just simple, but enjoyable.

Our Features

Dynamic Search

The Dynamic Search feature allows you to search movies and TV shows instantly using the OMDb API integration. You can enter any title, and the app will return a list of results with matching titles, including both movies and TV shows. It's an easy and fast way to discover content you're interested in.

Full Details

MyStream provides detailed information about each movie or TV show you explore. This includes plot summaries, genre, IMDb ratings, release year, poster images, and much more. With a simple click, you get all the information you need to decide whether you want to watch it.

Responsive Design

Our platform is designed to work seamlessly across all devices, including desktop, tablet, and mobile. The responsive layout ensures that you can access MyStream from anywhere and have a great experience regardless of the screen size.
